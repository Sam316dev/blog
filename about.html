About I LOVE CCM

A Nigerian kid who grew up on Don Moen cassette tapes and never stopped listening.

CCM has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. Growing up in Nigeria, I was that kid who knew every Don Moen song by heart before I really understood what worship even meant. The music just hit different. There was something about it that felt bigger than entertainment. It stayed with me.

Over the years my playlist got bigger. Those classic tape foundations expanded into incredible modern movements like Hillsong, Phil Wickham, Matthew West, and Elevation Worship.

I started I LOVE CCM because I wanted a place that actually takes this music seriously. Not just playlists and charts, but real conversations about the artists behind the songs, who they are, what drives them, and why their music connects the way it does. A space for people who love CCM the way I do.

If you've been listening since the Rich Mullins era or you just discovered CityAlight last week, you're in the right place. Pull up a chair.

Some of the artists we love around here:

Don Moen Michael W. Smith Phil Wickham Hillsong Maverick City Elevation Worship Casting Crowns MercyMe

New spotlights drop regularly. If there's an artist you think deserves a feature, we're all ears.
