About I LOVE CCM

A Nigerian kid who grew up on Don Moen and never really stopped.

CCM found me early. Growing up in Nigeria, Don Moen and Michael W. Smith were basically the soundtrack of my childhood. I knew their songs long before I fully understood what worship meant. Something about the music just got through in a way nothing else did, and honestly that has never changed.

I still listen to all of them. Don Moen, Michael W. Smith, Phil Wickham, Hillsong, Maverick City, Elevation Worship. The slow midnight worship songs and the loud Sunday morning praise tracks. I have never been loyal to just one sound within CCM because all of it means something to me in different ways.

Over time that love for the music went deeper than just listening. I started writing lyrics. In 2020 I had the opportunity to collaborate with Ark Anthems as a co-writer on a song called Silent, Not Absent, which is still available today. We also worked together on an earlier song called Somebody Needs Your Prayer which has since been taken down. Those two experiences showed me what it actually feels like to contribute to something that carries weight beyond just words on a page.

I have over 14 songs written, finished and unfinished, sitting with me right now. Getting them produced is a different journey with its own challenges, but the writing keeps going regardless. One day.

I LOVE CCM came out of all of this. I wanted a space that actually goes deep on the artists and songs that matter in this genre. Not just surface coverage but real spotlights on the people behind the music, what drives them, and why what they make connects the way it does. For people who love CCM the way I do, and for people just finding it.
