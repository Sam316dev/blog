Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This is a straightforward one. I LOVE CCM is a music blog. We are not a corporation, we do not run ads, and we are not in the business of collecting your data. This policy explains the one thing we do collect and exactly what we do with it.

What We Collect

The only personal information we collect is your email address, and only if you choose to sign up for our newsletter. We do not collect your name, location, phone number, or any other personal details. We do not track your behaviour on the site.

Why We Collect It

Your email address is used for one purpose only — to send you updates when new artist spotlights are published on I LOVE CCM. That is it. We will never use it for anything else.

What We Do Not Do

We do not sell your email address. We do not share it with any third party. We do not use it for advertising. We do not send spam. If you signed up for the newsletter, you will only ever hear from us when there is something new worth reading.

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time. Every email we send will include an unsubscribe link at the bottom. If you want your email address removed from our records entirely, just reach out and we will take care of it promptly.

Cookies

I LOVE CCM does not currently use cookies or any tracking technology. If that changes in the future, for example if we add analytics to understand how the site is being used, this policy will be updated to reflect that before any changes take effect.

External Links

Some of our posts include links to external platforms like Spotify or Apple Music. Once you leave I LOVE CCM, this privacy policy no longer applies. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We will not make changes that affect how your data is handled without letting you know first.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or want your email removed from our records, you can reach us at samueldahunsi6@gmail.com. We will always respond.
